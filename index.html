About

Currently studying Math & CS at Northwestern U


My background is in CS and Biology. Instead of attending high school, I worked for almost three years in a research team focusing on the mechanisms of repair of damaged DNA.

In college, I switched to mathematics and deep learning. Currently, I am pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Science and working on applications of deep learning for biomedicine and healthcare.
