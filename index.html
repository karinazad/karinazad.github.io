About

Currently studying Math & CS at Northwestern U


My background is in CS and Biology. Instead of attending high school, I worked for almost three years in a research team focusing on the mechanisms of repair of damaged DNA. Later, I realized that the best way to promote the biological research is by applying more computational approach.

In college, I switched to computer science and mathematics. Currently, I am working on applications of deep learning for biomedicine.
